üí° Key Innovation: Unlike black-box models, LumenAI first predicts interpretable clinical concepts (ABCDE criteria), then uses these concepts to make the final diagnosis - allowing doctors to understand and verify the AI's reasoning.

üìä ABCDE Criteria

Clinical Concepts

The model predicts 12 dermoscopic features that dermatologists use to identify malignant lesions.

üîÄ

Asymmetry

Measures shape irregularity. Malignant lesions often show asymmetry when divided by any axis.

üìê

Border Irregularity

Detects jagged, uneven, or blurred edges that are common in melanomas and other malignancies.

üé®

Color Variation

Identifies multiple colors (brown, black, red, white, blue) within the same lesion - a warning sign.

üìè

Diameter

Lesions larger than 6mm deserve attention. The model estimates relative size from image context.

üå´Ô∏è

Blue-White Veil

A specific dermoscopic pattern strongly associated with melanoma, appearing as a blue-white haze.

üï∏Ô∏è

Pigment Network

Analyzes the honeycomb pattern of pigmentation. Atypical networks suggest malignancy.
